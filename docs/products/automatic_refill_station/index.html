Automatic refill station

(Internet connected)


Register your interest

Automatically dispenses fluid into refillable bottles.

Choose your volume and walk away
User friendly, and ideal for stores fulfilling internet orders
Low energy consumption and small footprint
Can be flexibly positioned above a bulk fluid container. Avoids manual handling of bulk fluid containers above ground height.
Low level notifications and analytics
The refill station tracks how much inventory is left in your bulk container for automatic stock keeping.
Circular design
A substantial number of components in our design will be manufactured using recycled materials. We are committed to the principle of right to repair and our modular design allows components to be replaced easily.
