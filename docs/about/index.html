About us.
Our products reduce waste.

It is widely acknowledged that plastic pollution is a major cause of the global environmental crisis.

Globably each year we create and use 500 billion plastic bottles, that's an average of 66 bottles for each person on the planet. Less than half of these bottles are collected for recycling

Our products help reduce plastic pollution, by making it easier to refill bottles of cleaning products.

We're harnessing the power of modern robotic systems, and digital manufacturing methods to unlock business models resulting in lower waste.

Our machines enable low cost, local automation.
